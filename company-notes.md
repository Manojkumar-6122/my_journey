## 3. Ideabytes IoT

### Overview
Ideabytes IoT is an IoT technology company headquartered in Ottawa, Canada, with a development center in Hyderabad, India. The company develops IoT hardware and software solutions for monitoring critical environments.

### Products & Services
- Wireless data loggers
- Temperature and humidity monitoring
- IoT gateways
- Cloud monitoring platform
- Industrial automation
- Healthcare and pharmaceutical monitoring

### Industries Served
- Healthcare
- Pharmaceuticals
- Manufacturing
- Food & Beverage
- Cold Chain Logistics
- Agriculture

### Key Takeaway
Ideabytes IoT specializes in smart monitoring systems that provide real-time environmental data and help organizations maintain quality, compliance, and operational efficiency.
